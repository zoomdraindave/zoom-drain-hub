Privacy Policy

Effective: April 23, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Zoom Drain Phoenix ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you request services, including through third-party platforms such as Angi's. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Contact information — name, phone number, and email address
  • Service address — the location where service is requested
  • Service details — descriptions of the plumbing or drain issue you report
  • Communication records — call logs, text messages, and voicemails related to your service request
  • Lead source data — how your request reached us (e.g., submitted via Angi's)

We do not collect payment card numbers, Social Security numbers, or other sensitive financial identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Contact you by phone or SMS to respond to your service request
  • Schedule and confirm appointments
  • Send service-related updates and follow-up communications
  • Improve our internal response processes
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for unrelated marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

3. SMS and Telephone Communications

By submitting a service request through Angi's or directly to us, you consent to being contacted by Zoom Drain Phoenix via phone call and/or SMS at the number you provided, for the purpose of responding to your request.

SMS messages are transactional and directly related to your service request. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of SMS, reply STOP to any message. You will receive one confirmation and no further messages will be sent. Reply START to re-enable. Reply HELP for assistance.

Opting out of SMS does not affect our ability to contact you by phone about an active service request.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share it only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — Twilio processes phone numbers to deliver calls and messages on our behalf
  • Legal compliance — if required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger or acquisition

All third-party providers are contractually required to protect your information and may only use it to deliver services to us.

5. Data Retention

We retain your contact information and service records as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes in this policy or as required by law — typically up to three years for warranty, follow-up, and business record purposes.

You may request deletion at any time by contacting us (see Section 7).

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission, access controls, and secure storage. No method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, but we strive to use commercially acceptable protections.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP
  • File a complaint with a relevant regulatory authority

We will respond to all requests within 30 days.
