Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Zoom Drain Phoenix ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when requesting services, including information received through third-party lead platforms such as Angi's. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information transparently and responsibly.

01 Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Contact information — name, phone number, and email address
  • Service address — the location where service is requested
  • Service details — descriptions of the plumbing or drain issue you report
  • Communication records — call logs, text messages, and voicemails related to your service request
  • Lead source data — information about how your request reached us (e.g., submitted via Angi's)

We do not collect payment card numbers, Social Security numbers, or other sensitive financial or government-issued identifiers through this service.

02 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Contacting you by phone or SMS to respond to your service request
  • Scheduling and confirming appointments
  • Sending service-related updates and follow-up communications
  • Improving our internal response processes
  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not use your information for unrelated marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

03 SMS and Telephone Communications

By submitting a service request through Angi's or directly to us, you consent to being contacted by Zoom Drain Phoenix via phone call and/or SMS text message at the number you provided, for the purpose of responding to your request.

SMS messages we send are transactional in nature and directly related to your service request. Message frequency varies based on your request status. Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier.

To opt out of SMS communications, reply STOP to any text message you receive from us. You will receive a single confirmation message and no further SMS communications will be sent. To re-enable SMS, reply START. For help, reply HELP.

Opting out of SMS does not affect our ability to contact you by phone regarding an active service request.

04 How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — we use Twilio to send and receive calls and text messages on our behalf. Twilio processes phone numbers and message content solely to deliver communications.
  • Legal compliance — if required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of our business, your information may transfer to the acquiring entity

Any third-party service providers we work with are contractually required to protect your information and may only use it for the purpose of providing services to us.

05 Data Retention

We retain your contact information and service records for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. Service request records are typically retained for up to three years for warranty, follow-up, and business record purposes.

You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us directly (see Section 07). We will honor deletion requests promptly unless retention is required by law.

06 Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. These measures include encrypted data transmission, access controls, and secure storage practices.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

07 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • File a complaint with a relevant regulatory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information in Section 08 below. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.
